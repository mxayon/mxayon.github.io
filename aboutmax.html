Hello! I am Max! A Self-Actualized Product Designer, Cross-Dimensional Developer and Continuity Assembler. I am emphatic about measuring change, understanding the factors that drive things and thinking exponentially. I am a hueristic visual-spatial learner. A loyal product designer at heart, empathy prioritist at core, from start to finish and evolutionary stages.
I love the psychology behind tools and the design behind soultions being integrated. I like to find proof in real life examples. (my blog post on Turing)
In a birds-eye view of human-evolution - with life cycles in different scales, at the core of it all - logic, "Cogito Ergo Sum" means we create our own possibilities when we realize ourselves. We are propelled to sapiently construct ourselves into revolutionary cycles in different speeds and momentum. When objects collide in motion, the impact is cushioned if the objects - change or new develoment - are moving at their own constant speed. Being adapatable and knowing the behaviors of a tipping point is also a tool humans can look at to create a shared wisdom of adapting.
It is all in documenting where we came from to get some real evolving going on. Constantly re-evaluating the connections and how they intertwine in my pre-computer head helps the road to transparency light up.
I think there's a 4th dimension of depth that can provide some insight of how humans have evolved and react with machines. Looking at multiple date and time stamps zoomed out gives out a dizzying effect, but considering the different branches, renders the complete multi-dimensional image.
